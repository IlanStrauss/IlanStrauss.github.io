Current Projects

The Attribution Crisis in LLM Search Results: Estimating Ecosystem Exploitation
with Jangho Yang, Tim O'Reilly, Sruly Rosenblat, and Isobel Moure
Data for Policy
Forthcoming (Accepted)

How do LLM-powered search tools attribute (or fail to attribute) information to original sources? We estimate the scale of ecosystem exploitation when AI systems surface content without proper citation.

AI governance research focuses heavily on frontier risks and existential concerns. We identify critical gaps in research addressing AI safety and reliability in everyday consumer and enterprise deployments.

AI Disclosures Project

I co-direct the AI Disclosures Project with Tim O'Reilly, focused on architecting healthier AI markets through research, collaboration, and policy engagement. Key focus areas:

  • Open protocols and interoperability standards for AI
  • SEC disclosure requirements for AI systems
  • AI incident reporting and telemetry
  • Memory interoperability and data portability

Supported by the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation, Omidyar Network, and Patrick J. McGovern Foundation. We convene a Rockefeller Foundation Bellagio conference on AI market standards in April 2026.
