About

I co-direct the AI Disclosures Project with Tim O'Reilly, focused on architecting healthier AI markets through research, collaborations, and policy engagement.

I'm an Honorary Senior Research Fellow at UCL's Institute for Innovation and Public Purpose and a Visiting Professor at the University of Johannesburg (SARChI-ID).

My work on digital platforms and AI has been covered by Cory Doctorow, the Financial Times, TechCrunch, Fast Company, and presented to policymakers and AI labs including Anthropic. I serve on the editorial board of Data for Policy.
